MASE Photo Disclaimer

Math and Science Education (MASE) reserves the right to use any photograph/video taken at any camp, without the expressed written permission of those included within the photograph/video. MASE may use the photograph/video in publications or other media material produced, used or contracted by MASE including but not limited to: websites, flyers, social media, etc.

To ensure the privacy of individuals and children, images will not be identified using full names or personal identifying information without written approval from the photographed subject, parent or legal guardian.

A person attending a MASE event who does not wish to have their image recorded for distribution should make their wishes known to the photographer, and/or the event organizers, and/or contact MASE at mathandscienceeducation@gmail.com, in writing of his/her intentions and include a photograph. MASE will use the photo for identification purposes and will hold it in confidence. Additionally, if you wish to remove your image after the camp, you may also email us and we will take your photo down from our website.

By participating in a MASE event or by failing to notify MASE, in writing, your desire to not have your photograph used by MASE, you are agreeing to release, defend, hold harmless and indemnify MASE from any and all claims involving the use of your picture or likeness.

Any person or organization not affiliated with MASE may not use, copy, alter or modify MASE photographs, graphics, videography or other, similar reproductions or recordings without the advance written permission of an authorized designee from MASE.

Thank you for your understanding and cooperation!
